Frank and Ethel Oda opened up Hawai‘i Glass and Arts in 1946 and renamed the business Arts Hawai‘i in 1951. The beautifully hand drawn & etched glassware pieces by Frank Oda depicting flowers and foliage of Hawai‘i were very popular as wedding and special keepsake gifts for both visitors and kama‘āina (locals) in the 50’s and 60’s. Noted pieces were designed with etched koa handles. These rare hand drawn and etched glassware pieces by Frank Oda are highly collectible.
