Aloha! Angie and Jan are friends who live in Waimanalo, Hawaii and have been collecting for years. We are obsessed with vintage and keeping retro island style alive! You’ll often see us at different open markets and fairs in Hawaii driving around in our orange mobile boutique store!

Happy Hale feeds our soul, as this is what we love, the wonderful people we meet along the way as we share our island treasures and the joy of vintage!

Not only is vintage cool, but when you buy from Happy Hale, you support recycling and sustainability on our islands, you are buying a piece of island history that is ever so quietly slipping away, you get quality products from a time in place when people and things mattered, and lastly you get a unique product that is truly one of a kind at a great price.

Angie and Jan believe that you must be happy with what you’ve gotten from us. If at anytime you are not happy, please get in touch with us.
