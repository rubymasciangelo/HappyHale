Let us transport you to the romantic days of Hawai‘i, when life was slow and easy.

Hale means 'Home' in Hawaiian, and our collection is centered on joyful pieces for you and your home! Happy Hale specializes in all things made in Hawai‘i, mid-century Hawaiiana and collectibles. When you buy from Happy Hale you are buying a piece of island history that is ever so quietly slipping away, from a time in place when people and things mattered. Each purchase is an act of support for sustainability on our islands and adds a unique high-quality product to your home that is truly one of a kind.
