Our Story

Aloha! Angie & Jan are friends who live in Waimānalo, Hawai‘i and have been collecting for years. We are obsessed with vintage and keeping retro island style alive! You’ll often see us at different open markets and fairs in Hawai‘i driving around in our orange mobile boutique store! Happy Hale feeds our soul! We love to share our island treasures and the joy of vintage with the world!
